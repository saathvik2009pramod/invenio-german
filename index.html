About the App

No accounts. No pressure. Just effective learning.

NiveauDeutsch takes you through focused modules: notes, vocabulary with real pronunciation, practice questions, and reading comprehension , be it at A1, B1, or C1 level. Pick your path and start your journey!!.

A1
Beginner

Greetings, numbers, everyday verbs and basic grammar.

B1
Intermediate

C1
